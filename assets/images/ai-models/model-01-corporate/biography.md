# Biography

Fictional AI model archetype for polished professional portraits. The visual set works for career-focused demo profiles, office scenes, city-view portraits, and cafe lifestyle shots.

Suggested use:

- Marketing, software, fashion, or city professional seed profiles.
- Profile cards that need a calm, trustworthy first impression.
- Alternate poses for the same general corporate visual direction.

Do not describe this model as a real person. Keep public-facing copy clearly tied to seeded demo profiles.
