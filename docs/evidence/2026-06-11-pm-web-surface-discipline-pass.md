# PM_Web Surface Discipline Cleanup

Date: 2026-06-11
Owner: Codex

## What changed

- Reduced nested and repeated card-like surfaces in PM_Web `Hero`, `About`, `Download`, and `Membership` sections.
- Kept real grouped objects, such as the product preview and membership plan objects, while changing supporting details into rows, border strips, editorial lists, and section rhythm.
- Preserved launch-safe copy around waitlist-only access, no dating profile creation, no matching today, no checkout, and no payment collection.

## Why

The page should feel like an editorial conversion landing page, not a dashboard made of stacked boxes. This pass moves hierarchy back to typography, spacing, rows, and clear CTAs.
