## Design rule

Cards are allowed when they group one clear topic or object. Nested cards and repeated equal-weight panels are not the default hierarchy system.

PM_Web should read like a conversion-led landing page, not a dashboard of tiles.

PM_App should feel native and flow-led, using grouped rows, simple lists, sheets, typography, and spacing before extra boxed surfaces.
